IndiEDraCor


The Indian English Drama Corpus (IndiEDraCor) is a TEI P5 collection of Indian plays written in or translated into English, spanning the 1830s to the present. Public-domain texts are released here under CC0.

Scope and access
The corpus targets approximately 300 plays organised in two tiers:

Public tier. 
Texts in the public domain, encoded in TEI and released under CC0. These will be published on dracor.org.


Restricted tier.
In-copyright works encoded to the same DraCor standard but kept in a private DraCor instance for non-distributive scholarly use only.

Tier classification follows EU copyright rules (life + 70 years), since dracor.org is hosted in Germany. Plays migrate from restricted to public as their authors or translators enter the public domain.


Encoding of the remaining public-tier plays is in progress.
